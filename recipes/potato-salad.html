Potato Salad Recipe

A recipe for a creamy Potato Salad

This potato salad recipe will have everyone talking about it at the dinner table. Not only will people be dying for seconds, they will want to know this recipe as well.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Relish
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until tender
  2. Soft boil eggs
  3. Remove potatoes and eggs from heat
  4. Cut potatoes into bite size pieces
  5. Mix everything together in a bowl
  6. Chill until ready to serve

Lasagna Recipe

A recipe for a cheezy and meaty lasagna

This lasagna recipe will leave your friends and family craving for more. You want to use the freshest ingredients that will make it taste so much better. As always, you want to make it with love and delicacy.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the pan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
